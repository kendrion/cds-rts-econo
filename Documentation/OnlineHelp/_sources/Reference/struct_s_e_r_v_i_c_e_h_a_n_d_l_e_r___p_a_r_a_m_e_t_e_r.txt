Detailed Description
--------------------

**Return values**

+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ERR_OK            | Received service was completely handled.                                                                                                           |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ERR_L7_UNKNOWNCMD | Service is unknown by the event handler.                                                                                                           |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ERR_CALL_AGAIN    | Received service will be handled asynchronously. To progress this event will be posted again for the same received service data with bFirstCall=0. |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| ERR_...           | Another error occured, caller will send this result in a generic error tag as reply (or close the channel).                                        |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
