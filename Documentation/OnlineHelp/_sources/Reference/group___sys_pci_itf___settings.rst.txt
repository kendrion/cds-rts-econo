^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

<type>Int</type>

Address Offset of PCI addresses. This may be used for some VxWorks BSPs, which have 1:1 virtual:physical mapping in general, but a special mapping for PCI addresses. The offset is just added on the scanned PCI addresses.
