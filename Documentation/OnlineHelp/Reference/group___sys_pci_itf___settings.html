#define SYSPCIKEY_INT_VXWORKS_ADDRESSOFFSET “VxWorks.AddressOffset”¶

Address Offset of PCI addresses. This may be used for some VxWorks BSPs, which have 1:1 virt:phys mapping in gerneral, but a special mapping for PCI addresses. The offset is just added on the scanned PCI addresses.

#define SYSPCIVALUE_INT_VXWORKS_ADDRESSOFFSET_DEFAULT 0x0¶
