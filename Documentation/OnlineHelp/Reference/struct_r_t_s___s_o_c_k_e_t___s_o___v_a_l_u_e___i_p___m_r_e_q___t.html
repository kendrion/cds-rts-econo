7.1.5.671. RTS_SOCKET_SO_VALUE_IP_MREQ_T Struct Reference¶

#include “SysSocketItf.h”

Data Fields¶

  • INADDR imr_multiaddr

  • INADDR imr_interface

Detailed Description¶

Parameters for the socket options SOCKET_IP_ADD_MEMBERSHIP and SOCKET_IP_DROP_MEMBERSHIP to join/leave the socket to/from the supplied multi-cast group on the specified interface.

Field Documentation¶

INADDR RTS_SOCKET_SO_VALUE_IP_MREQ_T::imr_multiaddr¶

The address of the IPv4 multi-cast group.

INADDR RTS_SOCKET_SO_VALUE_IP_MREQ_T::imr_interface¶

The local address of the interface on which the multi-cast group should be joined or dropped.
