#define CO_NONE 0x0000¶

Options to specify properties of a connector

#define CO_SCAN_PENDING_SUPPORTED 0x0001¶

Scan the IO-configuration with segmented transfer is supported. In this case, the call of IoDrvScanModules() can return ERR_PENDING to get further calls to transmit more scan results.
