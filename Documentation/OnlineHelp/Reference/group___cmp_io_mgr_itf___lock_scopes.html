#define IOMGR_LOCK_READ_INPUTS UINT32_C(0x0001)¶

Scopes for mutual-exclusion locks to be used with the ui32LockScope parameters of the functions IoMgrLockEnter() and IoMgrLockLeave() .

Note: Use both locks IOMGR_LOCK_READ_INPUTS | IOMGR_LOCK_WRITE_OUTPUTS in the function StartBusCycle(). Lock critical sections in IoDrvReadInputs() and IoDrvStartBusCycle() .

#define IOMGR_LOCK_WRITE_OUTPUTS UINT32_C(0x0002)¶

Lock critical sections in IoDrvWriteOutputs() and IoDrvStartBusCycle() .
